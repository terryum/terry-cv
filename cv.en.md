## Summary

I build Physical AI systems that work in the real world. I started as an AI & robotics researcher, publishing 20+ papers cited over 2,000 times, then founded ART Lab to bring these technologies into everyday industries. The company was acquired by Cosmax, the world's leading cosmetics manufacturer, where I now lead AI & Robotics — applying Physical AI to manufacturing systems in which data is messy, environments are dynamic, and solutions must actually work.

## Research Interests

Physical AI reshaping industrial environments; the AI transformation of manufacturing; and tech startups that bring technology into real-world industries — grounded in robotics, deep learning, and real-world AI systems.
